Curriculum philosophy

Evergreen by design

The curriculum teaches principles, not tools. Commander's intent. Feedback loops. Output validation. Managing ambiguity. These are the skills that transfer regardless of which AI platform is dominant in 6 months.

The tools change. The judgment doesn't. That means the curriculum doesn't expire when the next model drops. LearnAIR can run cohort after cohort without rebuilding from scratch each time. That's the difference between a course and a program.
