How it sequences

sequential or parallel

A phased structure focused on veteran integration, revenue generation, and an incremental learning journey. Phases can run sequentially or in parallel depending on resources.

Phase 1

Create the veteran foothold

Connect the existing LearnAIR curriculum to veterans. Recruit through networks. Identify funding pathways. Begin the military overlay.

Phase 2

Build the vet outreach strategy

Develop a 2–3 hour engagement hook. Deploy across the veteran spectrum. Begin evolving the curriculum toward agentic content.

Phase 3

Agentic architecture

Build the agentic curriculum framework. Deploy to the broader LearnAIR community. Establish building blocks for the longer-term program.

Curriculum philosophy

Evergreen by design

Topical, not tool-based. Model-agnostic by design. Whether someone uses ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini is below the line. What we teach is above the line: the judgment layer that makes veterans effective supervisors of AI-enabled systems.

The tools change. The judgment doesn't. LearnAIR can run cohort after cohort without rebuilding from scratch when the next model drops. A scaffold that can also be repurposed for non-veteran audiences.
