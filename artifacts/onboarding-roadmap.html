Non-negotiable

Protecting trust

These are Paul's relationships. Every interaction reflects on him, not just on us. That's why we're sequencing instead of batch onboarding.

First impressions are permanent

A broken Oura connection or a silent bot after "hello" burns trust permanently. We get one shot per person.

Responsiveness over features

If someone messages and Milo doesn't respond for 6 hours, that's worse than no onboarding at all.

Wrong data is worse than no data

"I don't have that yet" is fine. A wrong insight from stale data is not. Graceful gaps, always.

Set expectations honestly

"This is early, text-only, you're helping us build this." That framing turns rough edges into features.
