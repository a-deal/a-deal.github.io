Position 2 / 20% Allocation
NVIDIA

The picks and shovels of AI. Every major AI company is their customer. CUDA has backward compatibility across 8 GPU generations, creating enormous switching costs. New chip architecture every 2 years keeps them ahead of competition.

I see compute demand firsthand. Every AI agent I build consumes GPU inference. The shift from training to inference scaling means more chips needed per query, not fewer.

Exit condition

Gross margins fall below 60% for 2 consecutive quarters, or custom silicon captures >30% of training market.

Position 3 / 20% Allocation

I'm personally on tirzepatide. I understand the patient experience, the pricing, the compliance challenges. Telehealth visits cost $50 vs $176 in-person. 3.6M Americans can't access care due to transportation barriers alone. HIMS is solving distribution, not inventing a drug.

Exit condition
