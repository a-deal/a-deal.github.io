Act 2

The one question

Minutes 20-40 The strategic heart of the call
What happens after someone checks off their habits?

Right now: nothing. Checkbox turns green. The user supplies all motivation. The system doesn't respond, doesn't learn, doesn't compound. Default-diverging.

Proposed: after check-in, the app shows 2-3 sentences from Kiso. Personalized. Based on real biometrics. "You hit bedtime 3 nights straight. Sleep is up 22 minutes. Resting HR dropped a beat."

This is the bridge. One endpoint. One UI element. One week. It connects habits to outcomes and makes the system feel alive.

Decision needed

Can we wire one feedback loop into the Today screen in week 1? What does SyncService need to call /api/v1/context? Show Paul the response for his real data on the call.

If yes: the sprint validates the intelligence thesis. If "let's get App Store first": that's fine, we have our sequencing answer.
