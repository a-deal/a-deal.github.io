Oregon Landscape: Veterans, AI, Employers, Funding

March 22, 2026

Top Findings

1 Oregon-NVIDIA $10M AI Partnership

Signed April 2025 by Gov. Kotek + Jensen Huang. AI Ambassador Program on campuses. K-12 AI integration. An AI operations supervisor program aligns directly with the state’s stated AI workforce priority.

2 East Cascades Works is the local workforce board

WIOA-designated entity for Bend/Deschutes County (10 counties east of Cascades). The right partner for federal workforce funding in Central Oregon. Headquartered in Bend.

3 Health Elements AI is in Bend

AI-powered clinical data abstraction. Real AI company, not just tech. BVC finalist. A concrete local employer for the “would you hire an AI-skilled veteran?” conversation.

4 Prineville data centers are 35 min from Bend

Meta (3.2M sq ft, $2B+ invested) + Apple (3.2M sq ft). ~300 permanent jobs combined. Infrastructure jobs that need supervision.

5 COCC already has veteran infrastructure

STRIVE Program (free 8-week veteran entrepreneurship, Syracuse IVMF partnership). Justin won STRIVE there. OSU-Cascades has CS with AI option. Neither offers AI operations training. Gap.

Veteran-Serving Organizations

Central Oregon (Bend area)

  • Central Oregon Veterans Outreach (COVO): 61510 S Hwy 97, Bend. Founded 2005. Homeless outreach, SSVF, Central Oregon Veterans Village (22-bed transitional). 200+ people/month.
  • WorkSource Bend: Nationally recognized for veteran services (NASWA award). Priority of Service for veterans.
  • 541VETS: Digital resource library for rural Oregon veterans (named for 541 area code)
  • East Cascades Works: Local workforce board for Central Oregon. Bend HQ. Partners with COIC, COCC, Oregon Employment Dept.
  • COIC (Central Oregon Intergovernmental Council): Operates WorkSource Oregon locally.

Statewide

  • Oregon Dept of Veterans’ Affairs (ODVA): JVSG ($2.4M federal), Military Skills Credentialing, Veteran Services Grant ($972K for 2025–27)
  • Easterseals Oregon: HVRP across 8 cities. Contact: hvrpreferrals@or.easterseals.com
  • Hire Heroes USA: Expanding into PNW. Partners with General Assembly (Project Forge, free tech training)
  • Oregon Employment Dept: LVERs and DVOPs at WorkSource centers statewide

Not in Oregon yet

  • VetsinTech: No confirmed Oregon chapter. Opportunity to start one.
  • Code Platoon: Chicago-based but fully remote option. GI Bill eligible. 15-week AI + Fullstack.
