Reading the Matrix

Three things the matrix communicates:

  1. Prong 1 (Literacy) catches the widest net. Enlisted and transitioning veterans are the volume play. This is revenue now with LearnAIR's existing curriculum adapted for military audiences.
  2. Prong 2 (Supervision) is the differentiated bet. Junior officers and warrant officers are the primary audience. Nobody else is training this cohort for this role. That's the gap.
  3. The funnel connects them. Prong 1 is the top of funnel. Veterans who show aptitude and interest in Prong 1 get invited to Prong 2. The heat map shows why both prongs exist: different audiences, different outcomes, connected pipeline.
