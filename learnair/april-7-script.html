Pre-Brief (9:15am, 15 min)

Energy check (1 min)

  • How'd you sleep? Scale 1-5. If either is below 3, compress to fast path.

Fast path decision (1 min)

  • Is Teresa confirmed for 30 min or short on time?
  • If short: skip Slides 5-7 (gap, urgency, structural). Go Thesis → Shift the Lens → Bridge → Aha Moment → Three Tracks → Asks.
  • If full 30: run the planned sequence.

Three reminders (2 min)

  • Stress test, not pitch. We're putting nine dots in front of them.
  • "Partner" needs to appear more than once. Reinforce at Three Tracks: "You've built the foundation. We're proposing to extend it."
  • Ask #2 is specific: "Are you interested in co-developing a Track 2 pilot?" Not just "level of interest."

Handoff protocol (1 min)

  • Mike speaks on all slides except navy (Urgency, Structural Problem).
  • Andrew: no interjections on Mike's slides. Wait for direct question or navy slide.
  • Mike pauses 2 seconds after each block. Andrew fills only if something big was missed.
  • If Justin asks about arrangement/structure: "Structure follows validation. We see this as a partnership."

Kill criteria (1 min)

  • Justin redirects everything into "how this fits our existing program" = they see us as a feature
  • Teresa asks about reporting structure or compensation = they're hiring, not partnering
  • "Let me think about it" without naming what needs deciding = polite no
  • None of these end the call. Just flag internally for debrief.

One dry run (5-8 min)

  • Mike reads the aha moment block out loud once. Not the whole deck. Just Slide 9.
  • Time it. Target 60-90 seconds. If it runs over 90, cut a sentence.
  • Andrew reads his urgency block once. Target 45 seconds.

Debrief (within 30 min of call)

1. What did they say yes to? (3 min)

  • Distinguish between explicit yes, implicit yes, and enthusiasm without commitment
  • Curriculum access: did they agree? With conditions?
  • Interest/commitment: did they name what they're deciding on, or was it vague?
  • Any offers we didn't ask for?

2. Where did energy shift? (3 min)

  • When did Justin lean in? When did he pull back?
  • Teresa: what landed with her specifically? What didn't register?
  • Was there a moment where they connected the tenth dot on their own?
  • Did anyone say something that surprised us?

3. Partnership vs. employee signal (2 min)

  • Did Justin use "partner" language or "team/build/join" language?
  • Did the "as you say" thread from Mike's email come up?
  • Did the structure conversation happen or get deferred?
  • Rate 1-5: how aligned is Justin's mental model with ours?

4. What did we get wrong? (2 min)

  • Any pre-mortem scenario that actually fired? How'd we handle it?
  • Did we talk too much on any slide?
  • Did the fast path / full path decision turn out right?
  • Did Andrew break the clean handoff protocol? Did it help or hurt?

5. Next actions (5 min)

  • Who sends the follow-up email? (Mike, within 24 hours)
  • What do we attach? The deck URL? The sharpening doc? Nothing?
  • Do we send the deck to Teresa separately?
  • When's our next touchpoint with Justin? Pin a date.
