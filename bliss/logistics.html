Event: Hoover Bio Leadership Summit, Traitel Building, Stanford. April 14, 2026, 9:00 AM – 6:30 PM.

Attire — non-meathead business casual for bigger build

Wear:

  • Dark button-down shirt (navy, charcoal, forest green, deep burgundy). Regular/classic fit, NOT slim.
  • Dark pants — wool trouser, navy or charcoal chinos. Avoid stone/khaki.
  • Leather shoes or clean leather sneakers, darker tones.
  • Belt matches shoes.
  • Optional: soft unstructured blazer or dark quarter-zip over the shirt for the reception.

Avoid:

  • Tight polos (reads athletic/meathead)
  • Short sleeves entirely (this crowd is long-sleeve)
  • Bright white shirt (high contrast = looks larger)
  • Horizontal stripes or busy patterns
  • Anything athletic-cut, compression, or performance-fabric

The rule that carries most of it: monochromatic darker tones top-to-bottom. Visually unified = looks grounded, not bulky. Also matches Hoover's default (serious-academic, not startup-casual).

Bag

Dark, slim profile. Not gym, not hiking. Leather, waxed canvas, or clean nylon work backpack. Everything zipped.

Do NOT assume bag check. Hoover Traitel is a small summit venue. Plan to carry it all day. Under your seat during panels. With you during breaks — a bag is also a prop. Carrying something reads less exposed than empty-handed.

What goes in the bag

  • iPhone charger + Pixel charger
  • Battery pack (≥10,000 mAh, verify it can charge iPhone while Granola records)
  • All cables
  • Water bottle (refillable — Stanford is dry)
  • 2–3 snacks: protein bar, nuts, apple, or whatever you prepped
  • Light layer for 55°F evening reception at Fairweather Courtyard
  • Wallet, ID, Clipper backup
  • Small notebook + pen (backup for hand notes; also signals "engaged" during panels)
  • Tissues + mints + hand sanitizer

What to LEAVE HOME

The laptop. You're not presenting. Granola is on iPhone, notes on Pixel, follow-ups wait until you're home. Laptop adds ~4 lbs, is another thing to worry about, and is a fidget-device that pulls you out of the room mentally. Leave it.

Two-phone hygiene

Smart split. iPhone = Granola recording. Pixel = everything else.

iPhone (Granola):

  • Do Not Disturb ON, all notifications suppressed during sessions
  • Airplane mode OFF (Granola may need connectivity)
  • Lock screen visible so you can see Granola is still recording
  • Keep battery pack connected throughout the day

Pixel (lifeline):

  • Primary for Uber, Clipper, maps, texts, web
  • Keep at 50%+ all day
  • Notifications normal

Battery pack priority: iPhone first (Granola drains fast), Pixel second.

Food

Breakfast at home. Protein-forward. Eggs, yogurt, protein shake — whatever your normal protein breakfast looks like.

Avoid: Heavy carbs (bagel, pastry) — they crash at 10am right when Window 1 hits.

Caffeine: Normal amount. Don't over-caffeinate — anxiety amplifier.

Snacks in bag: for the 2:50 break or whenever blood sugar dips.

Subway midday (if you decided to sneak out): use the 2:10–2:50 regional panel as cover. Walk out at 2:10, grab food, be back by 2:50 for the Kuiken panel at 3:20.
