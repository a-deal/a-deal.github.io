Feb 1, 2026
Cut started at 203.0 lbs. Target: 188.0. Hard stop: May 16.

20 years of training experience. Former personal trainer and gym owner. This isn't a first cut. The system is simple: caloric deficit with high protein, progressive overload, sleep as the foundation. 16 weeks max, then reverse diet regardless of where the weight is.

The Rules

2,400 cal on training days. Deficit is built in. Don't go lower.
190g+ protein. Non-negotiable. This is what preserves muscle in a deficit.
Sleep > everything. Poor sleep tanks recovery, raises cortisol, stalls fat loss. 7+ hours.
Hard stop May 16. 16 weeks is the limit. Reverse diet after, no exceptions.
